About Big RED

Out of Glen Cove, NY, the Big Red Food Truck has been feeding people breakfast sandwiches and lunches made from ingredients found locally and organically for many years.

Our mission is and always has been to provide the community with delicious and thoughtful meals using the best ingredients.

Come check us out!

Allison Prudente, CPC, started her Culinary Education as a child helping in her Grandmother's kitchen. Allison has attended classes at the Culinary Institute of America in Hyde Park, NY and the Natural Gourmet Institute in NYC.  In 2006 she received her degree from the Culinary Business Academy in Rio Rancho, New Mexico. In 2008, she proudly became a Certified Personal Chef with the United States Personal Chef Association.
